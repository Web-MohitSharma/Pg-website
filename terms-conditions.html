Terms of Stay

By choosing to stay at our PG, you agree to the following terms and conditions that ensure a smooth and respectful living environment for all residents.

General Terms

  • Residents must provide valid government-issued ID proof at the time of check-in.
  • Monthly rent must be paid in advance before the 5th of each month.
  • Security deposit is refundable upon checkout, subject to terms outlined on the Security Charges page.
  • Residents are responsible for maintaining cleanliness in their rooms and shared spaces.
  • Visitors are allowed only during permitted hours and must register at the front desk.
  • Illegal activities, possession of banned substances, or misconduct will lead to immediate eviction.

Cancellation & Exit Policy

  • A minimum of 7 days' notice is required before early check-out.
  • Rent for the ongoing month is non-refundable once paid.
  • Security deposit will be refunded within 7 working days after room inspection.

Liability & Conduct

  • PG management is not responsible for loss or theft of personal belongings. Use provided lockers for valuables.
  • Residents must not engage in activities that damage property or disturb other residents.
  • Any damage to furniture, walls, or appliances will be chargeable.

Other Linked Policies

Please also read our Privacy Policy, House Rules, and Support pages for a complete understanding of your stay and rights.
