Guidelines for Comfortable Living

To maintain a peaceful and respectful environment, all residents are expected to follow the rules mentioned below. These ensure everyone enjoys a clean, safe, and comfortable stay.

General Rules & Conduct

Quiet Hours

Maintain silence between 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM to respect others' rest time.

Cleanliness

Keep your room and common areas clean. Littering is strictly prohibited.

Guests

No overnight guests allowed without prior approval from the PG manager.

Kitchen Usage

Allowed during specific hours only. Clean up after use. Personal utensils must be labeled.

Security

Entry is allowed only via access card. Report lost cards immediately.

Electricity & Water

Use responsibly. Switch off fans, lights, and geysers when not in use.

Important Reminders

Violation of any rule may lead to a warning or expulsion based on severity. Management reserves the right to update rules periodically.
