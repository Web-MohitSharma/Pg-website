People usually ask these

How can I book a room?

You can book a room by filling out the booking form on our website or contacting us directly via phone or WhatsApp. Once your details are confirmed, we will reserve the room for you.

What documents are required during check-in?

At the time of check-in, you are required to provide a valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar card, PAN card, or passport), along with two passport-sized photographs.

Is food included in the PG charges?

Yes, we provide home-cooked meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner) as part of the PG package. The food menu is planned weekly and is nutritious and hygienic.

What amenities are provided?

We provide free Wi-Fi, RO drinking water, power backup, laundry facilities, CCTV security, daily cleaning, and furnished rooms with beds, wardrobes, and study tables.

What is the cancellation policy?

If you cancel your booking before check-in, a partial amount may be refunded depending on the notice period. After check-in, one month’s notice is required before leaving to avoid any deduction.
